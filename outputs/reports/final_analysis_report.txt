FINAL ANALYSIS REPORT
Monthly Rainfall Trend & Forecasting in Karnataka
Data: IMD Rainfall Dataset (1901-2015)
======================================================================

PROJECT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
This project analyzes 115 years of monthly rainfall data for
three Karnataka subdivisions using time series techniques.

SUBDIVISIONS ANALYZED:
  1. Coastal Karnataka (Western Ghats coast)
  2. North Interior Karnataka (Deccan plateau)
  3. South Interior Karnataka (transition zone)

MODELS IMPLEMENTED:
  1. Simple Moving Average (SMA)
  2. Simple Exponential Smoothing (SES)
  3. Holt-Winters Triple Exponential Smoothing
  4. SARIMA (Seasonal ARIMA) — Primary model
  5. Facebook Prophet
  6. LSTM Neural Network

KEY FINDINGS:
----------------------------------------

COASTAL KARNATAKA:
  Mean annual rainfall: 3406 mm
  Monsoon contribution: 87.5%
  Driest year: 1918 (2511 mm)
  Wettest year: 1961 (5554 mm)

NORTH INTERIOR KARNATAKA:
  Mean annual rainfall: 718 mm
  Monsoon contribution: 69.9%
  Driest year: 1920 (470 mm)
  Wettest year: 1997 (1096 mm)

SOUTH INTERIOR KARNATAKA:
  Mean annual rainfall: 1040 mm
  Monsoon contribution: 65.8%
  Driest year: 1918 (733 mm)
  Wettest year: 1961 (1410 mm)


DOMAIN APPLICATIONS:
----------------------------------------
  1. Kharif crop planning — sowing decision support
  2. Reservoir inflow estimation — water management
  3. Drought probability assessment — disaster preparedness
  4. Coastal vs Interior contrast — regional planning
  5. Climate change trend detection — policy support
